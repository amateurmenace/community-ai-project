Privacy & Data

We take privacy seriously. Here's how we handle data:

What We Collect

  • Minimal Analytics — We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our tools. This never includes personal information.
  • User-Provided Content — When you use tools like Community Highlighter, the content you submit (like YouTube URLs) is processed to provide the service. We don't store this content longer than necessary.
  • No Tracking — We don't use advertising trackers, sell data, or build user profiles.

Third-Party Services

Some of our tools currently use third-party AI services (like OpenAI or Google's Gemini) to provide functionality. When you use these tools, your queries may be processed by those services according to their privacy policies. We're actively working to transition to local LLM alternatives to reduce this dependency.
